B.B.A. (BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION)






The Bachelor of Business Administration courses offer an opportunity to acquire professional skills in the early stage of their career as a management professional. This undergraduate courses offers a deep understanding and development of important business skills such as leadership, communication skills, critical thinking, and decision-making.

This degree is designed to give a practical experience for the students in the form of projects, industrial visits, and interaction with the experts from the industry.

BBA program prepares the students to deal with the managerial issues and real life business problems but only at the initial level. A smart student chooses higher studies with specific specialization. A higher education program like MBA deals with the in-depth knowledge if the theories of business study. It helps in improving the person skills to bring out the leader from an aspirant.
