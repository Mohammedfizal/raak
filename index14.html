B.Com. (Bachelor of COMMERCE)






B.Com degree is structured to provide the students managerial skills in disciplines related to commerce. Also by the end of the program, students gain as in-depth knowledge on core subjects like accounting, law, statistics, finance, marketing, etc.

Students of B.Com can certainly expect high employment opportunities as today every business needs a commerce specialist or an accountant to run the business successfully. B.Com students have a good understanding of how the financial aspects of business need to be managed.
