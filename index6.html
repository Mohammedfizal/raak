BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATION






Bachelor of Computer Application (B.C.A) renders a sound academic foundation to the learners to help them pursue an excellent carrer in Computer Application and Information Technology. BCA is the perfect course for the students who have the desire to build their profession in Computer Science and relevant fields.

The sole objective of the BCA course is to provide an effectual and sound knowledge in the fundamental areas of Computer Science. It focuses on providing proficiency in developing software.

Career Opportunities in reputed IT companies like IBM, Oracle, Infosys as system engineer, junior programmer, web developer or as a system administrator etc. The government organization like NIC, Indian Army, Indian Air Force and Indian Navy also recruit large number of computer professionals for their IT department
