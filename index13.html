B.Sc. (Physics)






B.sc Physics educates the student with various aspects of matter, motion, energy, time and so on. Higher education after B.Sc Physics increases the proximity to get in to a high designation job with rewarding compensation packages.

The bachelor degree course in Physics acts as the base to study various higher education courses. The list of the possible courses after B.Sc physics are mentioned below.

  • Physics
  • Applied Physics & Ballistics
  • Applied Physics
  • Bio Physics
  • Engineering Physics
  • Geophysics
  • Maring Geo Physics Medical Physics
  • Renewable Energy

    Physics graduates can find ample career openings both in public as well as private sector enterprises. One can find various opportunities in governmental organizations like DRDO, VSSC, ISRO, SSPL, etc. They are also recruited in space research centers and in research laboratories
