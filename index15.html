B.com. (Corporate Secretaryship)






The course involves advance study of the concepts and processes of corporate governance with particular focus on the areas of Company Laws and secretarial practices. The courses curriculum consists of components of study such as corporate management practices, Investment management and economic legislation.

This program also help to gain knowledge on accounting, key cost concepts, Management accounts etc.,

Some areas of scope: Manager- Corporate/ Institutional Sales, Corporate Financial Analyst, Head- Corporate Development
