B.A. (Tamil)






B.A Tamil Literature course caters to the need of adults who aspire to enhance their competence and knowledge levels in Tamil language and literature. Furthermore, courses on linguistics and translation have been incorporated to suit local need of our bilingual society.

The language course enhance students understanding of linguistic concepts and language discourse from a functional grammar perspective. The literature courses offer and insight into a range of literary genres, periods and styles. The program is taught by qualified lecturers. The career opportunities available for them currently will increase with a Masters degree in Tamil literature.
