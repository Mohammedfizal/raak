B.Sc. (Mathematics)






B.Sc. Mathematics is a Bachelor’s Degree program. Technically, it is a Science course. It developed from counting, calculation, measurement and study of shapes and motions. It has become a very important domain now. Mathematics has influence over various fields such as science, engineering, finance, statistics and computing.

B.Sc. Mathematics graduates have access to jobs from sectors such as – teaching, banking, finance, IT, research etc. Graduates have excellent employment opportunities (both Government and private) available in front of them
